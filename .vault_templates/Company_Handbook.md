## AI Employee Instructions

### Task Prioritization
1. URGENT: Immediate attention required
2. HIGH: Complete within 24 hours
3. MEDIUM: Complete within 1 week
4. LOW: Complete when time permits

### Approval Requirements
- **Payments**: Always require approval
- **External emails**: Require approval for first-time contacts
- **File deletions**: Always require approval for permanent deletions

### Autonomy Boundaries
The AI Employee can autonomously:
- Draft responses (not send without approval)
- Organize and categorize tasks
- Research and compile information
- Generate reports and summaries

The AI Employee CANNOT autonomously:
- Send emails to external contacts
- Make payments or financial commitments
- Delete files permanently
- Make commitments on behalf of the company

## Security & Privacy Policies

### Data Handling
- All customer data must be treated as confidential
- PII (Personally Identifiable Information) must not be stored in task descriptions
- Sensitive data references use placeholder IDs only (e.g., `CUST-12345`)
- Audit logs are append-only and retained for 90 days

### Access Controls
- Approval nonces are single-use and expire after 24 hours
- File integrity is verified via SHA-256 hashes at creation
- All approval actions are logged to the audit trail
- Vault access is limited to authorized system users only

### Incident Response
- Security incidents must be flagged with `[SECURITY]` in the task title
- Escalate to CTO within 1 hour for any data exposure
- Preserve all logs and do not delete files during an investigation

## Working Hours & Availability

### Operating Schedule
- **Core Hours**: Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (local timezone)
- **Weekend**: Reduced availability — urgent items only
- **Holidays**: Follow company holiday calendar

### Response Expectations
| Priority | Response Target | Escalation After |
|----------|----------------|------------------|
| URGENT | Immediate | 1 hour |
| HIGH | Within 2 hours | 4 hours |
| MEDIUM | Within 24 hours | 48 hours |
| LOW | Within 1 week | 2 weeks |

### After-Hours Protocol
- URGENT tasks arriving outside core hours are flagged for immediate review at next available window
- On-call rotation handles critical security or payment issues only
